# Pre-work brief — {{session-name}}

What participants receive ~1 week before the session. Sent by sponsor, not facilitator (sponsor authority signals importance).

## Subject line (for the email)

## Greeting

A short personal note from the sponsor explaining why this session matters and why each invitee is being asked to attend. Three sentences max.

## What we're doing

> One sentence. The decision that will be made or the artifact produced. Not the topic.

## Why now

Two to three sentences on the timing. What's in motion, what window we're working inside, what changes if we don't do this.

This brief operationalizes Pattern 201 (*It starts before it "starts"*) and Pattern 203 (*Don't meet — MOBILIZE!*). The pre-work brief sent ~1 week ahead does more for session quality than any single agenda choice.
