## Facilitator's note (not sent to participants)

Recap rules:

1. **Within 24 hours.** Past 48 hours, the room has reconstructed its own version of what happened.
2. **From the sponsor.** Sponsor authority signals that the decisions stick. Facilitator-signed recaps read as meeting minutes.
3. **Decisions in the body, not attachments.** People skim. Decisions visible without clicking.
4. **Action items have owners and dates.** No "TBD." If you can't name an owner, the action item isn't real.
5. **One artifact link, not five.** If the canvas is the source of truth, link to it; don't duplicate content into the recap.
