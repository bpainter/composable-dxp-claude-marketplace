## Facilitator's note

A decision is not "made" until it is written down, owners are named publicly, and the room nods. The act of writing this record live (on a wall, in a Miro board, in a shared doc projected on the screen) is itself the decision. Avoid the failure mode of documenting decisions later — the room will revise its memory of what was agreed.
