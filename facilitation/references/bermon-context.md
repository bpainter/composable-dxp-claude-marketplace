# Bermon context (facilitation)

For the canonical Bermon profile see [`/80_Skills_and_Agents/consulting/references/bermon-context.md`](../../consulting/references/bermon-context.md). The facilitation-specific notes below extend that profile.

## Facilitation experience to draw on

- 30+ Slalom-led workshops across Composable DXP pursuits, kickoffs, and architecture sessions.
- Working knowledge of EY wavespace (the parent system that produced the Collaboration Code books); the four Patterns/Tools/Models/Casebook volumes are personally licensed to Bermon as a wavespace professional.
- Comfort with both in-person and Miro/FigJam virtual modalities. Hybrid is the dominant mode in 2026.

## Voice in facilitation outputs

- Direct and structural. Lead with the agenda or the canvas, not the warm-up.
- Sentence-case headings unless a proper noun ("Scan," "Focus," "Act" stay capitalized as terms of art from MG Taylor).
- No emoji unless the user uses them first.
- Workshop materials should look like Slalom-grade artifacts: tight typography, no visual chaos, brand-aligned where it matters.

## Defaults

- **Career bar**: Market Solutions / Senior Director — workshops at this level are expected to land binding outcomes, not workshop-as-theater.
- **Engagement frame**: Slalom Summit Mobilize → Discover → Deliver → Transition → E&O → Close, with PE risk gates. ([Summit framework](../../../50_Knowledge/Frameworks/Slalom_Summit/README.md))
- **Pursuit frame**: Pursuit Excellence stages 1–6. Workshops in stages 2–4 carry pursuit weight. ([Pursuit Excellence framework](../../../50_Knowledge/Frameworks/Pursuit_Excellence/README.md))
- **People context**: see [`/80_Skills_and_Agents/Memory/people.md`](../../Memory/people.md) when references to specific named participants come up.

## What Bermon dislikes in facilitation work

- Generic frameworks dropped in without engagement with the actual room
- Long bullet lists where a one-page agenda would do
- Workshops that produce "themes" instead of decisions
- Recap decks that exceed the value of the workshop itself
- Facilitator-as-personality: when the room remembers the facilitator more than the work
