The market is shifting faster than most playbooks can keep up

Three forces are converging — and most operating models weren't built for any one of them, let alone all three.

Customers expect the experience they get from their best digital service from every service. They don't grade you on a curve.

Internal systems are still organized by department. The data that used to be enough is no longer enough — and the data we do have is locked in places it can't move.

AI changed what's possible inside a quarter. Teams that adopt it as a workflow change move past teams that adopt it as a tool.

None of this is news. What's new is that the cost of waiting one more cycle now exceeds the cost of moving imperfectly.
