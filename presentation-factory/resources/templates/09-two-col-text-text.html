FROM VISION TO OUTCOME

What we set out to do — and what we delivered

THE VISION

A brand experience where fans co-create what they wear

Announcing new jerseys is a challenge for every kit supplier. Whether positive or negative, every fan has a strong opinion. AI Creator was designed to let them participate.

Together with Manchester City, PUMA wanted to combine AI technology with brand and fan culture — and activate fans everywhere in the world.

THE SOLUTION

A scalable infrastructure that handles 50,000 simultaneous prompts

Slalom built a global, scalable AI infrastructure on Google Kubernetes Engine, with a custom queueing system that sorts and buffers incoming user requests.

Design-generation time dropped from 60 to 17 seconds. The system needed 306 instead of 600 H100 GPUs — significantly more cost-effective.
