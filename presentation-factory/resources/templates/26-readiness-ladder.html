READINESS LADDER

From agent-visible to agent-executable

Platform-native toggles can create exposure fast, but durable differentiation comes from hardening enterprise data, APIs, identity, and fulfillment.

TIER 0 — INVISIBLE

Not discoverable by agents

No structured product data. Agents can't read or reason about your catalog. Search relies on copy.

Risk: competitors win the first impression.

TIER 1 — VISIBLE

Listed but not interactive

Product feed published. Agents can see SKUs. Cannot verify policy, inventory, or pricing in real time.

Outcome: surface area, not session.

TIER 2 — VERIFIABLE

Trusted by the agent

Real-time inventory, eligibility, and fulfillment APIs. Agent confidence high enough to recommend.

Outcome: routed demand share grows.

TIER 3 — EXECUTABLE

Agent completes the transaction

Cart, checkout, payment, and post-order service available via API. Agent finishes the journey on your stack.

Outcome: durable demand share.
