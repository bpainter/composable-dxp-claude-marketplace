## What we do

The Composable DXP practice sits inside Slalom's Enterprise capability. We help clients design and deliver composable digital experience platforms — modern, modular DXP architectures (CMS, commerce, search, personalization, identity, integration) wired together by APIs and event streams rather than monoliths. Sister practices in Enterprise cover Salesforce, Adobe Experience, ServiceNow, Anaplan / business planning, and integration (Boomi).

## Practice brand: The Future Is Composable

The practice maintains its own externally-facing sub-brand — **The Future Is Composable** (TFIC) — at `thefutureiscomposable.com`. Surfaces:
- TFIC microsite
- 2026 Composable Roadshow event materials
- CMO Playbook publication
- TFIC Insights articles and thought leadership

The sub-brand sits inside Slalom parent but **diverges intentionally** in voice and visual system. Use the sub-brand only on TFIC surfaces; everywhere else (proposals, firm decks, whitepapers, internal comms) the parent brand wins. ## Firm context (the bigger picture)

- Slalom is a digital consultancy. Strategy & Consulting (S&C) is organized into **seven capabilities**: Cloud-Build, CX, Data & AI, Delivery, Strategy, Transformation, Enterprise. See [[../../50_Knowledge/Frameworks/Slalom_Capabilities/README]].
- The firm runs on three intertwined frameworks the practice operates within:
  - [[../../50_Knowledge/Frameworks/Pursuit_Excellence/README|Pursuit Excellence]] — sales-stage discipline. Salesforce is system of record.
  - [[../../50_Knowledge/Frameworks/Delivery_Excellence/README|Delivery Excellence]] — quality and consistency on engagements.
  - [[../../50_Knowledge/Frameworks/Slalom_Summit/README|Slalom Summit]] — the integrated delivery methodology (Mobilize → Discover → Deliver → Transition → Enhance & Operate → Close, with 5 Risk Gates).
- Staffing per [[../../50_Knowledge/Frameworks/PE_DE_Roles/README|PE & DE Engagement Level and Roles]] — pairs me@Slalom Role Mastery Level with engagement complexity.
- 2026 firm sales goal: **$3B**. 2026 GTM-attributed target: **$600M (~20%)**. See [[../../50_Knowledge/Frameworks/Go_to_Market/README|Go-to-Market]].
