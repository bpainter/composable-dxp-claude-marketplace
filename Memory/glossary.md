# glossary.md

Acronyms, codenames, internal terminology. Helps Claude and ChatGPT decode shorthand without asking.

## Format

```
- **TERM** — definition. (optional: where it's used; aliases)
```

## Slalom firm-level

- **GBU** — Geographic Business Unit. Slalom's regional structure.
- **Capability** — one of seven firm-wide capability groupings (Cloud-Build, CX, Data & AI, Delivery, Strategy, Transformation, **Enterprise**). See [[../../50_Knowledge/Frameworks/Slalom_Capabilities/README|Slalom Capabilities]]. *Bermon sits in **Enterprise**.*
- **Practice** — a sub-grouping inside a capability (Bermon leads **Composable DXP** inside Enterprise).
- **S&C** — Strategy & Consulting (umbrella over the capabilities).
- **Track** — career track on the Career Navigator. Three tracks: **Client Delivery**, **Market Solutions**, **Account/Sales**. Bermon is on Market Solutions.
- **AD** — Account Director. Owns the client relationship.
- **CG** — Capability Group / Client Group meeting (context-dependent — usually pipeline review).
- **PCC** — Personal Contribution Credit. Revenue-contribution measure on the Career Navigator (applies to Market Solutions and Account tracks).

## Pursuit & Delivery (firm methodology)

- **PE** — Pursuit Excellence. Firm-wide sales-stage framework. See [[../../50_Knowledge/Frameworks/Pursuit_Excellence/README|Pursuit Excellence]].
- **DE** — Delivery Excellence. Firm-wide delivery framework. See [[../../50_Knowledge/Frameworks/Delivery_Excellence/README|Delivery Excellence]].
- **P&E Lifecycle** — Pursuit & Engagement Lifecycle. The end-to-end client journey from prospecting through delivery close.
- **Summit** — Slalom Summit. Firm's integrated, AI-powered delivery methodology with 6 phases (Mobilize, Discover, Deliver, Transition, Enhance & Operate, Close) and 5 Risk Gates. See [[../../50_Knowledge/Frameworks/Slalom_Summit/README|Slalom Summit]].
- **Risk Gate (RG1–RG5)** — exit-criteria checkpoints in Summit. RG1 ends Mobilize, RG2 ends Discover, RG3/RG4 inside Deliver, RG5 ends Close.
- **DQR** — Delivery Quality Review. Pause moment during delivery (esp. Complex engagements) where senior leaders review the work.
- **Deal Review** — the equivalent during pursuit.
- **Engagement Foundations** — the four constants on every engagement: joint kickoff, Customer Love Plan, retro, customer story.
- **Engagement Levels** — Low Touch / Standard / Complex (and similar tiers in PE & DE Roles guidance). Drives staffing model. See [[../../50_Knowledge/Frameworks/PE_DE_Roles/README|PE & DE Roles]].
- **RAIDD** — Risks, Assumptions, Issues, Dependencies, Decisions log. Maintained throughout an engagement.
- **SPRO** — proposal / pricing artifact uploaded into Salesforce as deals progress (latest by Stage 4).
- **Customer Love** — Slalom's term for client satisfaction / NPS-style program.

## Sales / Salesforce

- **SF** — Salesforce. The system of record for sales pipeline.
- **Sales Stage 1–6** — Prospect / Engage / Develop / Propose / Negotiate / Close.
- **Stage 0** — pre-Salesforce qualification work.
- **Probability** — gut-driven confidence rating; paired with Stage but distinct.
- **Impact Plan** — section of the Deal Strategy in SF where pursuit "wow" plans live.
- **GTM** — Go-to-Market. Slalom's quarterly drumbeat of priority solutions; 2026 target $600M (~20% of $3B firm goal). Hub: gtm.slalom.com. See [[../../50_Knowledge/Frameworks/Go_to_Market/README|Go-to-Market]].
- **GGP** — Global Growth & Partner support tag (used on opportunities).
- **Outlooks** — Slalom's annual industry/cross-industry POV release.
- **Zero Legacy** — GTM modernization campaign.
- **CCT** — Contact Center Transformation. GTM campaign anchored on Amazon Connect.

## Career & people

- **Career Navigator** — me@Slalom's career-expectation framework. Title × competency expectations across **Deliver Exceptionally / Grow Expertise / Grow Slalom / Lead**. See [[../../50_Knowledge/Frameworks/Career_Navigator/README|Career Navigator]].
- **me@Slalom** — Slalom's self-service HR/learning portal at me.slalom.com.
- **Role Mastery Level** — me@Slalom rating used in PE & DE staffing.
- **PEM** — Project Engagement Methodology / playbook (asset family referenced in Summit, e.g., "PEM – Discover", "PEM – Deliver").
- **Hypercare** — post-launch period of intensive support during Transition.
