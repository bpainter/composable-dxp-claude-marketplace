## What stayed in consulting

- `consulting-digital-strategist` cross-references the same DT books (Rogers, *Why DT Fails*, *Technology Fallacy*) with an engagement-shaping lens
- `consulting-change-management-advisor` owns OCM mechanics for innovation change work
- `consulting-management-consultant` handles the broader engagement structure when innovation is one workstream among several

## Why the split

The single `consulting-innovation-strategist` skill was overloaded — covering portfolio strategy, JTBD discovery, validation methodology, governance, lab design, capability building, and experimentation in one place. With the addition of source material from 14 canonical innovation books, the work earned its own plugin. See `innovation/README.md` for the full architecture.
